How to Play

Cast your line, wait for the lure, then click the shaking circles before they vanish. Each successful click adds 5% catch progress.

Steadiness now extends every shake's lifetime by 0.06 seconds per point. Luck shifts your fish roll toward rarer catches, while Haste shortens lure time.

Every successful catch can naturally receive one mutation. Mutations change its selling value, and some only appear at night.

Day and night each last ten real minutes. Certain Aurora Pond fish and rod bonuses only work during one period.

Most Unknown and Extinct fish are optional, but marked event creatures such as Megalodon and Godgazer are required.
